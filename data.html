Welcome to Iron Kondor Corporation, a leading Australian based international trading exchange / broker that helps traders & investors significantly grow their wealth. Our expertise comes from having worked for many years in banks and financial institutions globally. Iron Kondor was built by a team of financial services professionals that were sick and tired of having winning trades turn to losing trades in an instant due to irregular spread increases, stop hunting, enormous spikes, slippage, requotes and B-book practices of many Foreign Exchange (FX) brokers.


With the advent of Blockchain & Smart contract technologies, Iron Kondor Corporation was founded in early 2018, initially so that it could become a trustworthey & honest broker with its interestes alligned with those of traders and investors, and also to service it's sister company, Credience Corporation (www.credience.com). Very quickly, it was decided that Iron Kondor could also help many other traders & investors and so an ICO was launched.
